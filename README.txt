Track users to your website with your Greenrope CRM dashboard.

== Description ==

CRM Analytics pairs your Greenrope CRM account with your WordPress site so you can track new visitors. CRM Analytics is a must have for maximizing your company\'s marketing effort.

== Installation ==

1. Simply download the plugin and install.
2. Click the CRM menu item in the left menu.
3. Check \"Enable Tracking\".
4. Enter your Greenrope account number and the URL where your CRM account lives.
5. Save your options!
